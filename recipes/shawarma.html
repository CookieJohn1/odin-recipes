Shawarma


This famous meal is well known for it's rich recipes and flavor of its own. It's origin is in Otthoman Empire. Shawarma is a popular street food in the greater Middle East, including Egypt, Iraq, and the Levant, also served widely in Saudi Arabia.

Ingredients include:

  • Tahini
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Yoghurt
  • Flatbread
  • Lemon
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika

Steps to make one:

  1. Preheat a grill to medium-high heat. In the meantime, wash the chicken and drain excess water from the chicken pieces. Once, the chicken is moist, itâ€™s time to prepare the marinade
  2. Take a bowl and combine all the ingredients for marination. You can add your own choice of spices to make this dish even more delicious. Once the mixture is ready, rub it on the chicken pieces. To spread it properly, you can use your hands rather than using a spoon
  3. To prepare the sauce, combine yogurt with sesame paste, salt, garlic and mix well using a whipper. Then, barbecue the chicken strips until they are well cooked. If you are fond of the smoky flavours you can grill the chicken till it turns golden.
  4. Once done take pita bread and layer it with the sauce. Add pickled vegetable, cooked chicken and spread evenly on the bread. Now roll it or slice the pita bread with precision so that no rough edges remain. Serve the shawarma with green chutney and yoghurt sauce.You can also serve with a cheesy mayo
